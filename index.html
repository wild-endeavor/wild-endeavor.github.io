About

Hi and welcome! I am a developer with a background in finance and electrical engineering.

Before I moved out of New York this past March, I spent seven years on a mortgage trading desk doing research. It was fun, challenging, and interesting. Ultimately, over the protests of my former team and my closer friends, I wanted to leave to build things, to create and to innovate - in short, to do software development. Now in San Francisco, having finished development bootcamp class at App Academy, I'm actively looking for a junior software engineering position.

What's next? Hopefully something fast-paced, some place where I'm given the chance to prove myself with lots of elegant, production level code, shipping as often as new features are built. Over the years, I hope to gain a deep intuition for software, its design and architecture, to be able to play with code as a writer might play with words or a composer toy with notes to create that perfect counterpoint.

Current Projects

Books With Friends

 This is a personal interest of mine and offers functionality that goodreads.com currently doesn't. Over the years, as I borrowed and lent books to many friends, I lost track of which books were where and when I wanted them back and what I had personally borrowed, and from whom. I put this site together as an inventory management tool for those who do a lot of book sharing among friends. It relies heavily on the Google Books API and the associated search suggestion engine, and is built with Rails and Backbone.js. It's still a work in progress and there's a lot to be done. I will be continuing this once I have some free time again.

Trello Clone

 This is a light-weight clone of Trello. It uses jQueryUI to allow drag and drop of cards. Still a work in progress.
